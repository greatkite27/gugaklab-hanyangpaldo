Han Yunkyung

Pansori · Co-Director of GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO A pansori singer who delicately connects story and sound, expressing deep emotional layers through subtle vocal color.

Role · Co-Director, Pansori Singer
Focus · Full-length pansori, narrative development, vocal aesthetics

Han Yunkyung is a pansori singer who places great importance on the narrative flow and emotional structure of each piece. Her performances draw the audience into the story, balancing traditional vocal techniques with clear diction and expressive timing.

In GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO’s full-length pansori projects, she plays a central role in shaping the dramatic progression of the work. From quiet inner monologues to explosive climaxes, her vocal line traces the emotional trajectory of each character with precision.
