ARTIST

Kim Gwangyoon

Percussion · Co-Director of GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO A percussionist who leads the stage with powerful yet controlled rhythms, drawing from the energy of southern gut traditions such as Jindo Ssitgimgut.

Role · Co-Director, Percussionist
Focus · Gut rhythms, sinawi improvisation, ensemble leadership

Kim Gwangyoon specializes in traditional Korean percussion with a particular focus on southern gut rhythms including Jindo Ssitgimgut. His playing combines a solid grasp of traditional patterns with a modern sense of dynamics, shaping the overall flow of the stage through finely tuned energy control.

Within GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO, he functions not only as a performer but also as a musical director on stage, guiding the timing, tension, and release of each piece. His percussive language often becomes the axis that connects singers and instrumentalists.
