ARTIST

Moon Sangjun

Piri · Lead Artist of GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO A piri player who brings the aesthetics of stillness-in-movement into a contemporary context, weaving flexible yet solid melodic lines across sinawi and ensemble stages.

Role · Lead Artist, Piri Player
Focus · Sinawi, ensemble melodies, tonal color and phrasing

Moon Sangjun explores the expressive potential of the piri, focusing on the tension between stillness and movement in sound. His playing emphasizes subtle changes in tone, breath, and phrasing to create immersive melodic lines.

In GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO, he plays a key role in shaping the ensemble’s harmonic and melodic framework, often serving as the bridge between rhythm and voice. His approach to sinawi and ensemble improvisation highlights both personal expression and collective musical breath.

Selected Projects

  • · Sinawi Project – Live sessions with percussion and pansori
  • · Full-length Pansori Support – Piri-centered interludes and transitions
  • · Collaborative stages featuring experimental instrumentation
