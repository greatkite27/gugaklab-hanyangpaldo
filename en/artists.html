GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO brings together young Korean traditional musicians specializing in percussion, piri, and pansori. Each artist carries a distinct voice and musical color, and together they create a shared breath on stage where tradition and contemporary sensibility meet.

A percussionist who leads the stage with powerful yet controlled rhythms, drawing from the energy of southern gut rhythms including Jindo Ssitgimgut.
