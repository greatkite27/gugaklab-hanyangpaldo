ABOUT GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO

ABOUT

"The Essence of Korean Traditional Music Created by Young Musicians" GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO is an ensemble formed by emerging young Korean traditional musicians from top institutions such as Seoul National University, Hanyang University, Korea National University of Arts, and Chung-Ang University.

We strive to preserve and reinterpret the profound emotion and artistic beauty of traditional music from the eight provinces of Korea, expressing its unique color through the passion and creativity of the new generation.
