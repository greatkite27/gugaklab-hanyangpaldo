PROGRAM

GUGAKLAB HANYANGPALDO presents a wide range of performances, from full-length pansori and sinawi-based improvisation to repertoire series and collaborative projects. Each program is designed to explore both the depth of tradition and new possibilities for Korean traditional music.

2025
Full-length Pansori Project
Pansori

Kim Se-jong School Chunhyangga – “Unbreakable Virtue”

A full-length pansori performance following the lineage of traditional Chunhyangga.

This project presents the entire story of Chunhyangga as a full-length performance, allowing the audience to immerse themselves in the narrative and vocal color of traditional pansori. Through the interaction between the singer and drummer, the work unfolds with dense emotional and rhythmic tension.

Format: Full-length pansori · Live drum accompaniment

2025
Celebratory Performance
Folk Repertoire

23rd Korea Seodang Culture Festival – Celebratory Performance

A stage that celebrates the educational tradition of “Seodang” at the heart of Korean culture.

Centered on folk repertoire and celebratory pieces, this program was presented as part of the 23rd Korea Seodang Culture Festival. It highlights the musical spirit that has been passed down through local communities, connecting traditional education and performance.

Format: Folk repertoire · Ensemble performance

2024
Full-length Pansori Project
Pansori

Chunhyangga – “Unbreakable Virtue”

A full-length performance that captures the dramatic structure and vocal aesthetics of Chunhyangga.

Focusing on the narrative flow and melodic line of Chunhyangga, this stage explores the emotional depth of each scene while maintaining the traditional vocal style. The performance offers a concentrated experience of the charm of full-length pansori.

Format: Full-length pansori · Drum accompaniment
